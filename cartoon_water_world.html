🎓 WebGL vs Unity 对比学习

🌍 Scene (场景)
Unity: Scene文件 + Hierarchy面板
WebGL: new THREE.Scene() + scene.add()
📷 Camera (相机)
Unity: Camera组件 + Inspector设置
WebGL: new THREE.PerspectiveCamera(fov, aspect, near, far)
🎭 Material (材质)
Unity: Material文件 + Shader
WebGL: THREE.Material + GLSL Shader
💡 Light (光照)
Unity: Light组件 + Lighting窗口
WebGL: THREE.Light + renderer.shadowMap
